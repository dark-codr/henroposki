About Us

What is HSENG?

Henroposky Engineering Company Limited is a technology and service based establishment with exceptional expertise in planning, design, construction and maintenance of wide range of Civil, electrical and mechanical services. We also offer technical support services in the oil and gas downstream sector.

10+ Years Experienced.

With 10+ years in experience, Henroposky Engineering Company Limited is totally committed to providing products and services aimed at helping utility and industrial customers improve and sustain performance and productivity at a competitive cost.

Quality Services.

At Henroposky Engineering Company Limited, commitment to provision of quality services is characterized by honesty; loyalty and ethical business practices.

Mission.

Actively train and retrain our youths for active participation in the construction industry aimed at fostering an industrialized Nigeria.

Vision.

Henroposky Engineering Company Limited is envisioned to take Engineering and contracting services to the next level through indigenous technology and manpower development initiative.

Work Ethics.

Design and Construction of Buildings, Roads and General Civil Engineering Works. Our ability to Undertake certain kind of Jobs makes us conspicuous amonst many.
