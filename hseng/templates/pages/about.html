More than 10+ Years of Expirience.

Henroposky Engineering Company Limited is a technology and service based establishment with exceptional expertise in planning, design, construction and maintenance of wide range of Civil, electrical and mechanical works. It also offers technical support services in the oil and gas downstream sector.

Henroposky Engineering Company Limited is totally committed to providing products and services aimed at helping utility and industrial customers improve and sustain performance and productivity at a competitive cost.

At Henroposky Engineering Company Limited, commitment to provision of quality services is characterized by honesty; loyalty and ethical business practices.
